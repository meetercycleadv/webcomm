KedarKantha trek

Finest Summit Climbs in Winters

WHY TREK TO KEDARKANTHA

The summit climb of Kedarkantha is a very rewarding one. Right from the base camp of the trek, the summit looms large. As you start your trek in the early hours of the morning and steadily climb up the steep slope of snow, the world opens up around you. Outside the summit, the trek is also unique for its beautiful clearings. Very rarely you will find stunning clearings on a trek that makes way for some rest spots and beautiful campsites. This trek is filled with clearings on all the different routes

HIGHLIGHTS

What do you expect to see on this wonderful journey?

  • Beautiful Rhododendron forest
  • A lot of Snow
  • Mesmerising campsites
  • Adventurouse summit climb
  • Breathtaking views from Summit

ITINERARY

Day Trek Route
Day 1 Reach Sankri via Dehradun
Day 2 Trek From Sankri to Juda ka Talab (4 hrs trek)
Day 3 Trek from Juda ka Talab to KK Basecamp (5 hrs of trek)
Day 4 Summit Climb and back to Basecamp (5 hrs ascent, 3 hrs decent)
Day 5 Trek down to camp (4 hrs trek)
Day 6 Trek Down to Sankri (3 hrs trek)
Day 7 Depart for Dehradun
